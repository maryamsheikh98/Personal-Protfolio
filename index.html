About Me

Frontend Developer | Web Developer

Hi, I'm Maryam, a passionate web developer with a love for everything frontend! My journey into the world of web development started in 2024, and since then, I've been exploring and creating exciting projects. Whether it's crafting responsive layouts or bringing interactive elements to life, I find joy in every bit of frontend design.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (BSCS) at Federal Urdu University, and every day is an opportunity for me to learn something new. As I continue growing, my ultimate goal is to become both a full-stack developer and a creative frontend designer. I've already built projects like an image gallery, calculator, and portfolio, but I'm always looking to take on new challenges and expand my skills.

What defines my work style? I'm calm under pressure, creative with problem-solving, a perfectionist when it comes to the details, and a fast learner who enjoys collaborating with others. Teamwork is key, and I believe every project benefits from a collective effort.
